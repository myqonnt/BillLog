Privacy Policy

BillLog ("the App", "we", "our") is a personal bookkeeping application. This Privacy Policy explains what data the App may access, how the data is used, and what choices you have.

1. Data you create in the App

When you use BillLog, you may create or store:

  • Bills, income records, categories, tags, notes, and related bookkeeping content
  • Dashboard and board configuration
  • Optional location data attached to a record
  • Optional import and export files you choose to create or open
  • Optional web access credentials for the built-in local web server
  • Optional subscription status information returned by the App Store

This data is primarily stored on your device.

2. Data access and device permissions

BillLog may request access to the following device capabilities:

  • Face ID / device authentication: used to help protect access to the App
  • Location: used only when you choose to attach location information to a record
  • Local network: used only when you enable the built-in local web server so you can access your data from devices on the same network
  • iCloud / CloudKit: used only when you enable iCloud sync

If you deny a permission, the related feature may be unavailable.

3. How your data is used

We use data only to provide the App's features, including:

  • Recording and organizing bills and income
  • Displaying charts, dashboards, and reports
  • Syncing your data across your devices through iCloud when enabled
  • Exporting and importing your data when you request it
  • Verifying subscription status through Apple's App Store services
  • Protecting access to the App with device authentication

We do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking.

4. iCloud sync

If you enable iCloud sync, your data is stored and synchronized using Apple's CloudKit / iCloud services and may be transferred to and stored in Apple's infrastructure according to your Apple account settings and Apple's terms.

If you do not enable iCloud sync, your bookkeeping data remains stored locally on your device unless you choose to export it.

5. Local web server

If you enable the built-in web server feature, the App makes selected wallet data available on your local network to devices that can reach your device's local IP address and port.

This feature:

  • Runs only when you enable it
  • Is intended for use on your local network
  • Uses credentials that you set in the App

You are responsible for enabling this feature only in environments you trust.

6. Subscription and payments

Subscriptions and payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your full payment card details.

The App may access App Store subscription status data necessary to determine whether premium features should be available.

7. Crash and diagnostic information

The App may store limited local crash or diagnostic information on your device to help you review app stability. This information is not used for advertising or third-party tracking.

8. Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

We do not share your bookkeeping data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

Data may be processed by Apple when you use Apple services such as:

  • App Store subscriptions
  • iCloud / CloudKit sync
  • System review prompts

9. Data retention and deletion

You can delete records in the App at any time.

You can also remove exported files from your device and disable iCloud sync or local web access at any time.

If you uninstall the App, locally stored app data may be removed by the operating system. Data stored in iCloud may remain in your iCloud account until removed according to Apple's systems and settings.

10. Children's privacy

BillLog is not directed to children under the age required by applicable law for independent consent in their region.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the "Last updated" date above.

BillLog（以下简称“本应用”）是一款个人记账应用。本隐私政策说明本应用可能访问哪些数据、这些数据如何被使用，以及你可以如何管理相关权限与数据。

1. 你在应用中创建的数据

使用 BillLog 时，你可能会创建或保存以下数据：

  • 账单、收入记录、分类、标签、备注及其他记账内容
  • 仪表盘和看板配置
  • 你主动添加到记录中的位置信息
  • 你主动创建或选择的导入、导出文件
  • 你为本地 Web Server 功能设置的访问凭据
  • 由 App Store 返回的订阅状态信息

上述数据主要存储在你的设备上。

2. 应用访问的系统能力与权限

BillLog 可能请求以下权限或系统能力：

  • Face ID / 设备身份验证：用于帮助保护应用访问安全
  • 定位权限：仅在你选择给记录附加位置时使用
  • 本地网络权限：仅在你启用内置本地 Web Server 时使用，方便局域网设备访问你的数据
  • iCloud / CloudKit：仅在你启用 iCloud 同步时使用

如果你拒绝某项权限，对应功能可能无法使用。

3. 我们如何使用数据

本应用仅为提供核心功能而使用相关数据，包括：

  • 记录和整理账单与收入
  • 展示图表、仪表盘和报表
  • 在你启用 iCloud 同步后，通过 iCloud 在你的设备间同步数据
  • 在你主动请求时执行数据导入和导出
  • 通过 Apple 的 App Store 服务验证订阅状态
  • 通过设备身份验证保护应用访问

我们不会将你的数据用于广告投放，也不会进行跨 App 跟踪。

4. iCloud 同步

当你启用 iCloud 同步后，相关数据会通过 Apple 的 CloudKit / iCloud 服务进行存储和同步，并可能按照你的 Apple 账户设置与 Apple 的条款在 Apple 的基础设施中传输和保存。

如果你未启用 iCloud 同步，则除非你主动导出数据，否则记账数据会保留在你的本地设备上。

5. 本地 Web Server

当你启用内置 Web Server 功能后，本应用会在你的局域网内，通过设备的本地 IP 地址和端口向可访问该地址的设备提供部分账本数据访问能力。

该功能具有以下特点：

  • 仅在你主动启用时运行
  • 设计用途为局域网访问
  • 使用你在应用内设置的账号和密码

你需要自行判断并仅在可信网络环境中启用该功能。

6. 订阅与支付

订阅和支付由 Apple 通过 App Store 处理，我们不会获得你的完整支付卡信息。

本应用可能会访问用于判断高级功能是否可用的 App Store 订阅状态信息。

7. 崩溃与诊断信息

本应用可能会在你的设备本地保存有限的崩溃或诊断信息，用于帮助你了解应用稳定性情况。该信息不会被用于广告或第三方跟踪。

8. 数据共享

我们不会出售你的个人数据。

我们不会将你的记账数据共享给第三方广告商或数据经纪商。

当你使用以下 Apple 服务时，相关数据可能由 Apple 处理：

  • App Store 订阅
  • iCloud / CloudKit 同步
  • 系统评分提醒

9. 数据保留与删除

你可以随时在应用中删除记录。

你也可以随时删除导出文件，或关闭 iCloud 同步与本地 Web 访问功能。

当你卸载本应用后，设备本地保存的数据可能会由系统移除。若你启用了 iCloud，同步到 iCloud 的数据可能仍会按照 Apple 的系统机制和设置保留，直到被移除。

10. 儿童隐私

BillLog 并非面向未达到其所在地区独立同意年龄的儿童而设计。

11. 政策更新

我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。如有重大变更，我们会更新本文顶部的“最后更新”日期。
